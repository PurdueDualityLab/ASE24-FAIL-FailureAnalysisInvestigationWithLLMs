Oops! Bank pays back $45m
05/20/2006

ONE of Australia'sbiggest banks, the ANZ, has apologised to thousands of Mastercard users and promised refunds of millions of dollars for accidentally overcharging them.
The bank said yesterday it had refunded 200,000 customers a total of $45 million after a computer glitch double-dipped their accounts.
The malfunction affected400,000 transactions between 9pm on Friday last week, and 5pm Monday, charging Mastercard customers twice. The error, detected on Tuesday, only affected non-ANZ Mastercard holders using ANZ EFTPOS facilities.
