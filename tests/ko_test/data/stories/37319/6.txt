ANZ Bank apologises for overcharging 200,000 Mastercard users
05/19/2006
Asia in Focus
fv
MELBOURNE, May 19 Asia inFocus - The ANZ BANK (ASX:ANZ) has apologised to 200,000 Mastercard users and will refund millions of dollars it overcharged them by accidentally double-dipping their accounts. Spokesman Paul Edwards said the "embarrassing" glitch affected a total of 400,000 Mastercard transactions with a value of $A45 million ($US34.32 million) between 9pm (AEST) last Friday and 5pm on Monday.
* The error, which was detected on Tuesday, only affected non-ANZ Mastercard users.
* "It's extra embarrassing for us because we've impacted on people who aren't even our customers," Mr Edwards said.
SUMMARY
Australia's ANZ Bank to refund millions of dollars it accidentally overcharged 200,000 Mastercard customers
