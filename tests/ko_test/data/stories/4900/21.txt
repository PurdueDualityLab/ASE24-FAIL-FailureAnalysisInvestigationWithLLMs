The government urged the nation's 23 million small businesses Tuesday to prepare for the Year 2000 'millennium bug' by consulting a World Wide Web site designed to help them cope with the computer glitch. The Small Business Administration said it had created a Year 2000 page accessible from its home page at http://www.sba.gov.offcampus.lib.washington.edu/ to help companies decide whether they face the problem and determine how to grapple with it.
