MON INCLINE IN SERVICE
03/18/1999
Pittsburgh Post-Gazette (Pennsylvania)*Pittsburgh Post-Gazette
fv
The Monongahela Inclinewas running again today yesterday after workers fixed mechanical problems described by officials as a "computer glitch."
The incline, a popularPittsburgh tourist attraction, usually has its first run around 5 a.m. and carries its last riders at about 2 a.m. the next morning.
