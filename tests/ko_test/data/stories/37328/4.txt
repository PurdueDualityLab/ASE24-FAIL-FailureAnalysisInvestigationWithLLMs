Palm Treo 700w ROM update now back
04/29/2006
jkOnTheRun
ln
Apr. 29, 2006 (jkOnTheRun delivered by Newstex) --
Palm has once again released the AKU2 ROM update for the Treo 700w phone. They have fixed the glitches in the install process and have stated that if you have already updated with the first download you do not need to do it again.
