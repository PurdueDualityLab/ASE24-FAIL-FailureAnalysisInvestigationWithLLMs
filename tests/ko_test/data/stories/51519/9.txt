Disruptions to ATMs caused by systems outage: IBM
07/07/2010
TODAY (Singapore)
fv
Monday’s islandwide DBS-POSB ATM disruptions were the result of a systems outage said the bank’s vendor IBM.
Responding to queries from MediaCorp, IBM said it worked with DBS immediately to restore services. Meanwhile, a DBS spokesman said the bank is reviewing and revising its resilience and redundancy plans.
More than 1,000 ATMs were down on Monday from as early as 3am following the technical glitch.
DBS said that as of 10am on Monday, all branch and ATMs had resumed normal services. The bank is now conducting a full scale investigation into the incident.
On Monday night, DBS group chief executive Piyush Gupta had ruled out any act of sabotage. S Ramesh
