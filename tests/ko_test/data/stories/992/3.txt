No time set for resumption of NYMEX metals trading.
06/15/1993
Reuters News
fv
NEW YORK, June 15, Reuter - No time has been set for the resumption of precious metals trading at the New York Mercantile Exchange (NYMEX), an exchange spokeswoman said.
Trading was halted for the second time today due to a technical glitch in price dissemination.
"No time has been set yet," the exchange spokeswoman said.
Trading in NYMEX energy contracts resumed at 1130 EDT/1530 GMT.
