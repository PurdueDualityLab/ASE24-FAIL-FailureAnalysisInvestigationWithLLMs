Comex says trading to resume at 1035 edt/1435 gmt.
06/15/1993
Reuters News
fv
NEW YORK, June 15, Reuter - Trading at Commodity Exchange Inc (COMEX) will resume at 1035 EDT/1435 GMT, an exchange spokesman said.
Trading was halted at about 0945 EDT/1345 GMT due to a technical glitch in price dissemination, the spokesman said.
