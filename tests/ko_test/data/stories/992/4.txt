NYMEX trading closed due to price reporting glitch.
06/15/1993
Reuters News
fv
NEW YORK, June 15, Reuter - Floor traders said trading in the New York Mercantile Exchange's (NYMEX) energy and precious metals contracts was closed due to a price reporting problem.
A NYMEX spokeswoman said "We are down again."
She had no information on when trading will resume.
The opening of trading in energy and precious metals contracts was delayed due to the problem with price reporting.
