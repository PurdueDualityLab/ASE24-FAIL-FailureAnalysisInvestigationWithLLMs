COMEX says will resume trade at 1215 edt/1615 gmt.
06/15/1993
Reuters News
fv
NEW YORK, June 15, Reuter - Commodity Exchange (COMEX) Inc will resume trading in all its markets at 1215 EDT/1615 GMT, with regularly-scheduled closing hours for each market, an exchange spokesman said.
"We will resume trading in all markets at 1215," the spokesman said. "We'll have regular closing hours."
COMEX halted trading at about 0945 EDT/1345 GMT and resumed trading at 1035 EDT/1435 EDT only to cease operations again at 1045 EDT/1445 GMT due to a technical glitch in price dissemination.
