Glitch prevents interest payments
01/13/2010
Scotsman
ln
NATIONAL Savings and Investments warned yesterday that 8,000 customers had not received interest payments so far this month due to a technical glitch.
The government-backed group said the fault meant that payments which should have been made to certain Income Bond holders on 5 January had not yet gone through.
