A JUNGLE OUT THERE
01/23/1995
WALL STREET JOURNAL
ln
Frederick Rose and Richard Turner article discusses Hollywood's awkward flirtation with computer technology; focuses on glitches associated with Walt Disney Co's CD-ROM version of 'The Lion King,' which generated many complaints about incompatibility and inadequate technical support; notes that Disney has been fitfully trying to master the software business since 1988 and now plans to build its computer-software division to a $1 billion business within five years (L)
