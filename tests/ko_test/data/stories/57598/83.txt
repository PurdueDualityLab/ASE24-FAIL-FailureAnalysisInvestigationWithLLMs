Tokyo bourse glitch fixed, normal trade from 0330 GMT
02/01/2012
Reuters News
fv
TOKYO, Feb 2 (Reuters) - The Tokyo Stock Exchange saidon Thursday that a system glitch that hit trading has been fixed and normal trade will resume at 12:30 pm (0330 GMT).
The exchange said trade in 241 instruments had beensuspended due to the glitch, including shares of Sony Corp , Tokyo Electric Power Co and Hitachi Ltd .
(Reporting by Mayumi Negishi; Editing by Edwina Gibbs)
