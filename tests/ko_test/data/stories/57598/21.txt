TSE Confirms Arrowhead Glitch, Unsure When Trading Will Resume
02/02/2012
Nikkei Report
fv
TOKYO (NQN)--The Tokyo Stock Exchange said Thursday that a glitchin its "arrowhead" trading system prompted it to suspend trading of 241 issues that morning, and that it is unsure when trading will resume.
After noticing that thesystem was not distributing information on stock prices, orders and other data, the bourse suspended trading at 9 a.m.
The TSE still does notknow what caused the problem and said it will make an announcement as soon as it decides when it will resume trading.
The 241 issues include common stocks, exchange-traded funds and real estate investment trusts.
This is the first trouble associated with arrowhead since its launch in January 2010.
