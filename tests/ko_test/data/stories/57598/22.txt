Sapporo Bourse Halts All Trading Due To TSE Glitch
02/02/2012
Nikkei Report
fv
TOKYO (NQN)--The SapporoSecurities Exchange on Thursday said it suspended trading of all of its 74 listed stocks at 9 a.m. due to a problem with the Tokyo Stock Exchange's trading system.
The cause of the system glitchhas not been identified and it remains unclear when the TSE's system will be fully restored. It is also not clear when the Sapporo bourse will resume trading, but the exchange said that it will conduct after-hours trading as usual.
Trading remains normal on the Osaka Securities Exchange, the Nagoya Stock Exchange and the Fukuoka Stock Exchange.
