TSE system glitch suspends trading for 241 issues
02/01/2012
Kyodo News*Japan Economic Newswire
fv
TOKYO, Feb. 2 -- The Tokyo Stock Exchange said Thursday it has temporarily halted the trading of 241 issues due to a glitch in its market data distribution system.
Trading in the issues has been halted since 9 a.m., the bourse said, adding that it is investigating the cause of the glitch.
