UPDATE 1-Glitch forces Turkish bourse to cancel morning session
03/02/2012
Reuters News
fv
(Updates with Bourse announcement)
ISTANBUL, March 2(Reuters) - Turkey's Istanbul Stock Exchange said on Friday it cancelled the morning trading session due to a technical problem and that and all transactions and orders placed before live trading was due to start had been cancelled.
The Bourse didn't specify when trading would resume.
