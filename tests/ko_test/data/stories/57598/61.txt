TSE Suspends Trading in 241 Stocks, ETFs, Bonds due to Glitch
02/01/2012
Jiji Press English News Service*Jiji Press Ticker Service
fv
Tokyo, Feb. 2 (Jiji Press)--The Tokyo Stock Exchangesaid Thursday it suspended trading in a total of 241 stocks, exchange-traded funds and corporate bonds from 9 a.m. (midnight Wednesday GMT) due to a computer system glitch.
Among the stocks subject to the trading halt are Sony Corp. , Hitachi Ltd. , Tokyo Electric Power Co. , Aeon Co. and Tokyo Gas Co. , the TSE said.
A problem occurred in the TSE's market data distribution system, according to the exchange.
