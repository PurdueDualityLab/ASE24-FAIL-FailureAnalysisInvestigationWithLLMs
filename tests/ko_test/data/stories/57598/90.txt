TOKYO, Feb 2 (Reuters) - The Tokyo Stock Exchange saidit would brief at 0730 GMT on a system glitch that had suspended trade in 241 instruments including shares of Sony Corp, Tokyo Electric Power Co and Hitachi Ltd.
