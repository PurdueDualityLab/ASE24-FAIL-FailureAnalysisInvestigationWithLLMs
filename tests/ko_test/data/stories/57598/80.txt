TSE to Resume Trading in 241 Halted Issues at 12:30 P.M.
02/01/2012
Jiji Press English News Service*Jiji Press Ticker Service
fv
Tokyo, Feb. 2 (JijiPress)--The Tokyo Stock Exchange said Thursday it will resume trading in the 241 stocks and other securities affected by a glitch in its computer system at the start of the day's afternoon session at 12:30 p.m. (3:30 a.m. GMT).
Trading orders will be accepted from 12:05 p.m., the exchange said.
Investors will be givenenough time to change or cancel the orders they placed before the trading suspension for the affected securities, the TSE said.
Due to the trouble,trading was suspended at 9 a.m. for the 241 issues--222 stocks, 12 exchange-traded funds, two real estate investment trusts and five convertible bond issues.
