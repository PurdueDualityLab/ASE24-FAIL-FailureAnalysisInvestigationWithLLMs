Japan/United States: Nikkei opens mixed ahead of U.S. job data
02/06/2012
Thai News Service*Asia News Monitor
pq
Tokyo stocks opened mixed Friday ahead of the release of a key U.S. jobs data with the key Nikkei index down 0.21 percent.
At 9:15 a.m., the 225-issue Nikkei Stock Average lost 18.53 points from Thursday to 8,858.29.
The broader Topix index expanded 0.78 point, or 0.10 percent, to 763.23.
The Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) returned to normal operations after it was affected by a glitch in its market data distribution system on Thursday, which led to suspended trading of 241 issues in the morning. - PNA
