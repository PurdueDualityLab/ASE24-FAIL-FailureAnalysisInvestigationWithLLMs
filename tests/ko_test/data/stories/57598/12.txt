Trading on Istanbul bourse to resume at 2 pm after glitch
03/02/2012
Reuters News
fv
ISTANBUL, March 2(Reuters) - Turkey's Istanbul Stock Exchange said on Friday trading will resume at 2 p.m. local time (1200 GMT) after it cancelled the morning trading session due to a technical problem.
All transactions and orders placed before live trading was due to start had been cancelled.
(Reporting by Seda Sezer)
