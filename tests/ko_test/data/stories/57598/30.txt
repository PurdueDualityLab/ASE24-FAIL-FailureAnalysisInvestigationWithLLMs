TSE Mulling Executive Pay Cuts Due to System Failure
02/08/2012
Jiji Press English News Service*Jiji Press Ticker Service
fv
Tokyo, Feb. 9 (Jiji Press)--Tokyo Stock Exchange Inc.is considering cutting executive compensation to take responsibility for the trading system failure last week, informed sources said Thursday.
The pay cuts will be partof a report on the specific cause of the trouble and in-house punitive measures the operator of Japan's largest stock exchange is expected to submit to the Financial Services Agency next week, the sources said.
On Feb. 2, the TSE suspended trading in 241 stocks including Sony Corp. and Tokyo Electric Power Co. for the entire morning session, due to a transaction information system glitch accompanied by failure of backup servers.
