RUSH-HOUR RAIL CHAOS
09/30/2005
Daily Record*Scottish Daily Record
fv
COMMUTERS faced travel chaos as a computer glitch hit rail services yesterday.
All trains in and out of Edinburgh Waverley were axed after a signalling fault.
Services from GlasgowQueen Street were cut to two an hour and ended at Linlithgow, where passengers were told they had to make their own way to the capital.
More road traffic led totailbacks on the Forth Road Bridge and main routes to Edinburgh. Services were back to normal after 9am
