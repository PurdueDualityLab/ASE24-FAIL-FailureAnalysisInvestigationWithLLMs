Air traffic control hit by radar  glitch
03/16/2010
The Herald*The Herald (Glasgow)
ln
AIRCRAFT movements in Scotland and northern England were temporarily restricted last night after a 40-minute radar glitch . Air traffic control agency NATS said all planes were in voice contact with control towers. Flights into airports within the Manchester area control centre were affected, but a spokeswoman was unable to say how many.
