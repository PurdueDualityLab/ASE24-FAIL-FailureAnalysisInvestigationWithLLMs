Radar glitch; ROUND-UP
03/16/2010
Daily Post (Liverpool)*Daily Post
ln
AIRCRAFT movements in northern England and Scotland were temporarily restricted last night after a 40-minute radar 'glitch', air traffic control agency NATS said.
Flights into airports within the Manchester Area Control -north of the Midlands and Scotland - were restricted immediately after the problem emerged at 5.50pm.
